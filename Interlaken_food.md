## Tips for Dining in Interlaken

1. **Reservations recommended** for popular restaurants, especially dinner
2. **Cash is still widely accepted** but credit cards are common
3. **Prices are in CHF** - Switzerland is expensive by European standards
4. **Lunch menus** often offer better value (CHF 11-15)
5. **Many restaurants close** on Mondays or Tuesdays
6. **Self-service options** like Migros offer the best value
